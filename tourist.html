When discussing the most popular tourist destinations in Asia, China, with over sixty-five million tourists visiting the country every year, tops the list. Alongside China, other popular tourist destinations in Asia are the Philippines, Malaysia, Thailand, Singapore, South Korea, Japan, Indonesia, and Taiwan. According to the latest reports on the most popular tourist destinations globally, China appears fourth, with Thailand following eighth.


The Philippines is also highly positioned, with around eight million international travelers visiting the country every year. The Philippines is also known for well-established casino tourism, with the best Philippines casinos accommodating thousands of locals and international visitors daily. In the following sections, we explore the country's rising casino tourism and the different factors that make the Philippines one of Asia's most popular tourist destinations.


Tourism in Asian countries offers the most spectacular artificial and natural wonders. Visitors from Europe and other parts of the globe get to see some of the most impressive architectural achievements when visiting China, India, Thailand, and the Philippines. Asian countries also house some of the most spectacular beaches, crystalline waters, and tropical rainforests.
According to the latest reports, tourism and travel industries in Asia have significantly grown over the last several years. Due to improved connectivity, both tourism and travel revenues have increased in the region by around 5% to reach a value of over US$2,9 billion as estimated in 2019. Essentially, tourism and travel industries account for over 9% of the region's GDP (gross domestic product).
The Philippines is a trendy casino tourism destination in Asia, and land-based casinos can be found in many major cities. One of the most famous casino resorts in the Philippines is Solaire Resort and Casino Manila, located in the country's capital city. Casino Filipino Pavillion and Casino Bacolod are equally renowned. Land-based casinos can also be found in Angeles City, Cebu City, Clark Freeport, Davao City, Lapu-Lapu, and Laoag City.
